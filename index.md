## About Me

I am a Postdoctoral-level researcher specializing in Speech Processing and NLP for Low-Resource Languages. With a PhD in Computer Science and over 12 years of experience across academia and industry, my work focuses on speech recognition, translation, and multilingual NLP. I leverage deep learning and self-supervised models on HPC/Cloud infrastructures to build reproducible, production-ready systems. My career includes leading nationally funded research grants, publishing in top-tier journals, and driving international collaborations to advance linguistic AI.

## Research Interests

- **🗣️ Speech Processing:** ASR, TTS, and Voice Cloning for low-resource languages
- **🌐 Multilingual NLP:** Cross-lingual transfer and self-supervised learning
- **🔁 Machine Translation:** Neural MT and multimodal representation learning.
- **🏗️ AI Infrastructure:** Scaling deep learning on HPC and NVIDIA DGX clusters.
